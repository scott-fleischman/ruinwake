## 100% Completion Walkthrough

### Phase 1: First Night Survival (Pos 0, 0)

1. **Spawn** at The Shire (0, 0). Talk to **Hal the Ranger** nearby at (10, 8) — press T.
2. Punch grass and dirt to gather **fiber** and **dirt**. Break leaves for **sticks**.
3. Open crafting (E) and make **Sticks from Wood** (1 wood → 4 sticks).
4. If you have coal (dig down a few blocks), craft **Torches** (1 stick + 1 coal → 2 torches).
5. Build a simple 4-wall shelter with a roof before dusk — shelter quality protects at night.
6. **Survive the night** → Quest objective "Survive the first night" completes automatically on Day 2.

**Discoverable nearby:** Cache at (15, 10) — gives 3 bread. Lore Book at (10, 5) — gives 1 lore_fragment.

### Phase 2: Chapter 1 — Embers in the Wild

7. Head to the **Ruined Watch-Post** at **(30, 30)**.
8. Reaching it triggers "Reach the ruined outpost" objective.
9. Gather **15 wood + 10 stone** (use an axe for wood, pickaxe for stone — craft at workbench first).
10. Press **R** near the ruin to restore it → "Activate the ward fragment" completes.
11. **Chapter 1 complete!** Chapter 2 unlocks. +50 XP.

**Discoverable:** Roadstone at (60, 15) — 3 stone.

### Phase 3: Chapter 2 — Broken Roads

12. Travel east to **Bree** area around **(80, 22)**.
13. Talk to **Old Tom the Innkeeper** at (85, 22) — press T to accept quest.
14. Go to the **Roadside Ranger Hall** at **(90, 25)**.
15. Gather **20 wood + 15 stone + 5 iron_ore** (dig underground for iron).
16. Press R to restore the hall. +100 XP, +50 road_wardens rep.
17. Explore the corruption area around (100, 30) — walk through 3 times to complete "Investigate corruption signs."

**Discoverable:** Cache at (85, 18) — 5 iron_ore. Roadstone at (120, 25) — 3 stone.

### Phase 4: Chapter 3 — The Hidden Records

18. Travel east to **Rivendell** at **(200, 30)**.
19. Reaching the area triggers "Reach the Rivendell region."
20. Talk to **Elara the Lorekeeper** at (200, 35) — "Learn about the ward network" completes.
21. Find the relic at (205, 30) — walk near it for auto-discovery. "Obtain the first true relic" completes.

**Discoverable:** Lore Book at (205, 30) — 2 lore_fragment.

### Phase 5: Chapter 4 — Beneath the Mountains

22. Head to **Misty Mountains** at **(270, 50)**.
23. Reaching the area triggers "Enter the Misty Mountains delve."
24. Talk to **Thorin the Smith** at (275, 55).
25. Go to the **Dwarven Mountain Workshop** at **(270, 55)**.
26. Gather **30 stone + 15 iron_ore + 10 copper_ore**.
27. Press R to restore → "Stabilize the dwarven mechanism" completes.
28. Kill enemies in the area around (265, 45) → "Defeat the corrupted guardian."

**Discoverables:** Inscription at (280, 50) — 1 lore_fragment. Recipe Tablet at (270, 52) — 1 lore_fragment.

### Phase 6: Chapter 5 — The Dark Wood Stirs

29. Travel to **Mirkwood** at **(400, 40)**. Warning: corruption zone — fog turns green!
30. Entering triggers "Enter Mirkwood."
31. Talk to **Galawen the Warden** at (395, 40).
32. Kill spiders around (400, 45) — need 3 kills for "Clear spider nests."
33. Go to the **Mirkwood Forest Beacon** at **(400, 45)**.
34. Gather **25 wood + 5 relic_shard + 15 fiber**.
35. Press R to restore → "Restore the forest beacon."

**Discoverable:** Relic Fragment at (410, 45) — 2 relic_shard.

### Phase 7: Chapter 6 — The Sleeping Forge

36. Travel to **Erebor** at **(520, 20)**.
37. Reaching triggers "Reach the ancient forge."
38. Craft at a forge station near (520, 20) → "Unlock final crafting tier."
39. Find the discoverable at (515, 25) → "Discover the awakening source location."

**Discoverable:** Cache at (515, 20) — 10 iron_ore.

### Phase 8: Chapter 7 — The Rekindled Shadow

40. Travel to **Dol Guldur** at **(420, 90)**. Strongest corruption zone!
41. Entering triggers "Assault the corrupted stronghold." Beware: magic costs 50% more here.
42. Go to the **Ward Bastion** at **(500, 30)**.
43. Gather **40 stone + 20 iron_ore + 10 relic_shard + 5 lore_fragment** (hardest restoration).
44. Press R to restore → "Disable the network link."
45. Kill the champion enemy near (425, 95) → "Defeat the dark champion."

### Phase 9: Chapter 8 — After the Night (Epilogue)

46. Return to the starter area at **(30, 30)** → "Return to a restored settlement."
47. Walk around the Shire (0, 0) → "Explore the renewed world."
48. **Main quest complete!** Continue sandbox play.

## Crafting Quick Guide

### Without a Station (Hand Crafting)
- 1 wood → 4 sticks
- 3 fiber → 1 rope
- 1 stick + 1 coal → 2 torches
- 2 wood → 4 planks

### Workbench (place a workbench block, stand near it)
- 2 stick + 3 stone → Stone Pickaxe
- 2 stick + 2 stone → Stone Axe
- 6 fiber + 2 leather → Bedroll
- 2 stick + 3 iron_ingot → Iron Pickaxe

### Forge
- 2 iron_ore + 1 coal → Iron Ingot
- 2 copper_ore + 1 coal → Copper Ingot
- 2 iron_ingot + 1 stick → Iron Sword
- 3 iron_ingot + 2 stick → Iron Hammer

### Campfire
- 1 raw_meat → Cooked Meat (+25 hunger, stamina regen buff)

### Kitchen
- 2 cooked_meat + 1 mushroom → 2 Hearth Stew (+40 hunger each!)

### Loom
- 5 leather + 2 rope → Leather Armor

### Rune Table (magic crafting — requires relic shards)
- 2 relic_shard + 4 stone → Ward Stone (corruption resistance)
- 1 relic_shard + 2 glass + 1 copper_ingot → Elvish Lamp (light source)
- 3 relic_shard + 1 iron_sword + 1 lore_fragment → Runic Blade (enhanced sword)

## Skill Tree Priorities

Spend skill points (earned from leveling up) in Tab menu. There are 6 trees:
**Survival**, **Combat**, **Craft**, **Exploration**, **Fellowship**, **Attunement**.

**Essential early skills:**
- **Survival → Hardy** — Slower hunger drain (huge quality of life)
- **Craft → Material Yield** — More materials per block mined
- **Combat → Parry** — Timed parry for melee defense
- **Exploration → Pathfinder** — Faster fog-of-war reveal

**Mid-game priorities:**
- **Craft → Advanced Recipes** — Unlock intermediate crafting
- **Combat → Heavy Blow** — Stronger heavy attacks
- **Fellowship → Barter** — Better NPC trade rates
- **Attunement → Focus Pool** — More relic ability uses

## Enemy Threat Guide

| Threat Level | Enemies | Strategy |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Low | Wolf, Goblin, Spider | Basic melee works |
| Medium | Orc, Giant Spider, Goblin Archer | Use guard (G), watch for ranged |
| High | Troll, Wight, Corrupted Bear | Kite and use relic abilities |
| Very High | Guardian, Dark Construct, Barrow Wight | Full armor + iron weapons needed |
| Extreme | Wraith, Shadow | Fast and deadly — use Ward Light relic |

Enemies spawn at dusk/night. Better shelter = fewer spawns.
